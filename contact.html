Get In Touch With Us

Nirmal Dham is located in Chhawla Village, which lies on the Western edge of the residential sub-city of Dwarka in Delhi. It is also directly located on an extension of the UER(Urban Extension Road)-2 which provides good connectivity by road to the National Capital Region of Gurgaon(now Gurugram). Visitors can come by the Delhi Metro uptil the Dwarka Sector-21 station, from where a free shuttle service to Nirmal Dham, as well as other means of transport like autorickshaws are conveniently available. The site is easily traceable on Google Maps and can be navigated to, or set as destination if travelling by personal vehicles/taxi cabs.
