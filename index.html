About Sahaja Yoga

Sahaja Yoga is a unique method of meditation introduced in 1970 by Shri Mataji Nirmala Devi by which Self Realization (Kundalini awakening) is achieved effortlessly. Through this process an inner transformation takes place by which one becomes moral, united, integrated and balanced. It opens the door to a new dimension in human awareness, whose benefits are experienced by hundreds of thousands worldwide.

About Shri Mataji

Shri Mataji Nirmala Devi is the founder of Sahaja Yoga. She is the first and only one to offer the experience of self-realization en masse. She declared that it is the birthright of all human beings - regardless of religion, race, nationality or circumstance - to receive their self-realization. She also insisted that one cannot pay for the truth or for self-knowledge, thus Sahaja Yoga and its techniques have always been, and continue to be, offered free of charge.

About Nirmal Dham

Nirmal Dham is the collective ashram and one of the main meditation centres for the people of Delhi NCR. Since its completion in the year 2000, it has been the venue of several national and international pujas, many graced by Shri Mataji herself. It has also been the resting place of our Holy Mother since 23rd February 2011, when She decided to leave the physical aspect of Her most auspicious Incarnation.
